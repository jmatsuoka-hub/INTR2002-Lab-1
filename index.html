Directions:

  1. Fill a Pot with 6 cups (1.5L) of water


  2. Place the pot on the stove and bring to a boil
  3. Carefully add pasta to the boiling water
  4. Cook for 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally until pasta reaches desired tenderness
  5. Using the collander, drain the water and placed cooked pasta back into the pot (do not rinse pasta!)


  6. Add 2 tbsp of butter or margarine, 1/3 cup of milk, and cheese sauce mix
  7. Mix well until pasta is evenly coated
  8. Serve and enjoy!
